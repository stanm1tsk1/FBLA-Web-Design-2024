Booking Information

How to book:

  1. Click the "Booking/Rentals" tab on the navigation bar
  2. Click the type of event that you would like to make
    • There are only four types of events so please choose the one that accuratly depicts the even that you would like to hold
  3. Click the number of hours you would like your event duration to be
    • The maximum duaration is 48 hours
    • Our arena can only host a maximum capacity of 3,500 people so please plan accordingly
  4. Click submit
    • Your Done! Please wait for our email confirming your booking
